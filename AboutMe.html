About Me

I am currently a student in the second year of the engineering cycle (M1) at ECE Paris. Having an interest in health, I joined the Magisterium in Biology-Health University Paris-Sud XI. I chose to finish my education in engineering school, at the ECE Paris, with the aim of acquiring knowledge in computer science and electronics, and thus to equip me to develop technological innovations in e-health and biotechnologies. As my studies and internships progressed, I developed a strong interest in subjects of oncology and artificial intelligence. This translates into a curiosity pushing me to follow the news in this area. I am convinced that the combination of biology, robotics and applied computing will solve a large number of diseases and health problems.
